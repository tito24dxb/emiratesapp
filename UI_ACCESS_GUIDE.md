## ğŸš€ Step-by-Step: First Time Setup

### As a Seller

#### **Step 1: Create Your First Product**
1. Click **"Seller Dashboard"** from sidebar
2. Click **"Create New Product"** button (blue, top of page)
3. Fill in product details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Price
   - Upload images
   - (Optional) Custom CTA button text
4. Click **"Publish Product"**

#### **Step 2: View Your Dashboard**
- Path: `/seller/dashboard`
- See all your products in table format
- View stats: Products, Views, Sales, Revenue

#### **Step 3: Check Your Earnings**
1. Click **"My Earnings"** from sidebar or navbar
2. Path: `/seller/billing`
3. View:
   - Total earnings
   - Transaction history
   - Customer details
   - Process refunds

---

### As a Buyer

#### **Step 1: Browse Products**
1. Click **"Marketplace"** from sidebar
2. Browse product grid
3. Use search and filters

#### **Step 2: Contact Seller**
1. Click any product
2. On detail page, click **"Contact Seller"**
3. Chat window opens (bottom-right)
4. Type message and press Enter

#### **Step 3: Share Products**
1. On product page, click **"Share"** button
2. Choose:
   - **"Copy Internal Link"** - for community chat
   - **"Share Full Link"** - for external sharing
3. Paste in community chat

#### **Step 4: Purchase**
1. Click **"Buy Now"** (or custom CTA)
2. Fill billing details
3. Complete payment
4. Receive confirmation

## ğŸ› ï¸ Troubleshooting

### "I don't see Seller Dashboard"
**Solution**: Scroll down the sidebar - it's below Marketplace

### "I don't see the chat"
**Solution**: Chat only appears after clicking "Contact Seller" on a product page

### "Contact Seller button is disabled"
**Solution**: You're viewing your own product - you can't message yourself

### "I don't see My Earnings"
**Solution**: Look in sidebar or navbar profile menu - it's labeled "My Earnings" not "Billing"

### "Share button doesn't work"
**Solution**: Make sure you're on a product detail page, not the marketplace grid
